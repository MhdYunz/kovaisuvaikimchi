Kovai Suvai Kimchi — Authentic Korean home-cooking

Owners: Mohmed Yunus & Femina

We started as a home cloud kitchen delivering Korean dishes to friends and neighbours. Now we run a small pushcart and continue to make homestyle kimchi and Korean street bites — inspired by Femina's love for K-drama and Korean cuisine.

We focus on: fresh kimchi, kimbap, tteokbokki, jajangmyeon-style fusion & seasonal Korean side dishes.
